About Me

Hello! My name is Marti McElreath and I’m a recently-hatched web developer. When I’m not spending my time coding, I enjoy playing pool, drinking beer, and reading weird fiction.

I lived in Japan and Korea for over seven years and speak both languages fluently, and my most recent passion project is Russian. I’m always a month behind on my Economist and Scientific American subscriptions, but mindfulness and yoga have taught me how to forgive myself.

My goals in becoming a web developer are to challenge myself intellectually and to achieve a degree of career flexibility that would allow me to run half-marathons all over the world. Someday I might even get a dog.
